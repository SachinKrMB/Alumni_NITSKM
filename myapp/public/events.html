Spotlight Events

Udgam

Udgam is the cultural fest of NIT Sikkim, celebrating creativity through dance, music, drama, and art. It brings together students and artists nationwide for lively competitions, performances, and workshops, showcasing culture and youth.

Convocation

Convocation is a formal graduation ceremony where degrees and honors are awarded. It celebrates students' academic achievements and marks their transition from student life to their future endeavors.

Abhiyantran

Abhiyantran is the technical fest of NIT Sikkim, featuring competitions, workshops, hackathons, and seminars. It brings together tech enthusiasts to showcase skills in coding, robotics, and innovation, fostering creativity and collaboration.

Induction

Induction is an introductory program for new students to familiarize them with the campus, its culture, and academic life. It includes sessions on university policies, resources, student activities, and mentorship opportunities.

SIH (Smart India Hackathon)

Smart India Hackathon (SIH) is a nationwide competition encouraging students to develop innovative tech solutions for real-world challenges.
